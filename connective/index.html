Custom-tailored Solutions

We work with you to create a solution that is perfectly suited for your industry and your customers. Mix and match from a variety of features we can offer.

One-to-many Streaming

We allow you to “bring your own CDN” and integrate with most content delivery networks to allow you to use existing infrastructure to distribute streams

Many-to-many Streaming

We can also provide “rooms” and conference call solutions in which many users are streaming at once

Native Mobile Apps

Our platform is compatible with Android and iOS and we can support native app implementations

One-to-many Chat

We allow for fully customizable asynchronous chat

Many-to-many Chat

Add in a full user list and private messages for an even more functional chat

Interactive Rooms

We keep track of room state, allowing for features like contextual interactions, raised hands, and polls

Share Screen or Canvas

Give your users the ability to share their screen or even stream any canvas directly to support tools like whiteboards and annotations

Analytics

We can track user interactions, and provide analytics on bitrate, CPU, etc. to ensure the best possible user experience

Customizable User Settings

Control how your users can interact during sessions with fine grain control over customizable settings
